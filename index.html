Welcome To
Ascension !

Welcome to the website for the Catholic Church of the Ascension, Bodija. We are located in Ibadan, Oyo State, South-West Nigeria.

Ours is a decidedly historic community. With Right Reverend Monsignor Patrick Ugboko being the pastor at the time, the church building was dedicated by His Lordship Bishop Felix Alaba (Ibadan Diocese) on the 15th Day of May, 1993 in honour of the ascension into heaven of our loving Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.
