You Are Welcome To
Ascension!

This is the official website for the Catholic Church of the Ascension, Bodija. We are located in Ibadan, Oyo State, South-West Nigeria.

Ours is a decidedly historic worshipping community. With the pastor of that period in the person of Right Reverend Monsignor Patrick Ugboko, our church building was dedicated on the 15th Day of May, 1993 by His Lordship Bishop Felix Alaba (Ibadan Diocese) in honour of the ascension into heaven of our loving Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.
